What is Toggl?

Toggl is an app designed to turn your everyday social media usage into a learning opportunity. Every time you open a restricted app, Toggl challenges you with a quick question from your selected learning mode, keeping you engaged and informed.

Why Toggl?

  • Personalized Learning: Choose from modes like SAT Tutor, Coding Practice, and Relaxation Exercises.
  • Efficient Time Use: Make every moment count by turning idle browsing into quick learning sessions.
  • Stay Accountable: Track your progress and usage statistics to stay motivated.
  • Fun and Interactive: Earn rewards and badges as you learn!
