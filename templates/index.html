What is Toggl?

Toggl is an innovative app that transforms how you use your screen time. Every time you access a restricted app, Toggl presents a quick learning question tailored to your selected mode.

Personalized Learning

Choose from Interview Prep, General Trivia, MCAT Study Mode, and more. Or, build a custom study mode! Toggl adapts to your preferences, making learning part of your routine.

Track Your Progress

Toggl will track your learning progress and encourage you to keep limiting the endless scrolling. Earn badges and rewards while tracking your learning milestones.

Privacy Policy

Toggl is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information. As part of this commitment, we do not track, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) without your explicit consent. All app data, settings, and content are securely stored locally on your device. To enhance the functionality and quality of Toggl, we may collect limited, anonymized data to optimize user experience and app performance.
